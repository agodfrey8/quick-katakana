The Three "Alphabets"

Kanji

Writing in Japan came from China. Many existing Japanese words were given a Chinese character called kanji in Japanese. These are the complicated pictograms people think of when they think Japanese is a difficult language.

  • 魚 – fish
  • 水 – water
  • 木 – tree

Chinese spoken language was very different from Japanese though. The characters only represented the Japanese meaning. Later, a Japanese writing system called man’yogana was created by using Chinese characters for their phonetic meaning only.
